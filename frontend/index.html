How Bayanihan Connect Works

Simple steps to give or receive help

1

Create Your Account

Register as a Requester, Helper, or both. Add your location, skills, and profile photo.

2

Post or Browse Requests

Requesters describe what they need. Helpers browse and filter by category, urgency, or location.

3

Connect and Coordinate

Helpers send an offer; requesters accept. Both parties communicate via the built-in chat.

4

Resolve and Recognize

Mark the request resolved and rate your helper. Top helpers earn badges and appear on the leaderboard.
